🔐 Privacy Policy

🐾 Quizzy — made for families
📅 Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Quizzy (the "App"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of our young users and their parents. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information within the App — with transparency and child safety as our top priorities.

2. Children's Privacy

COPPA & GDPR compliant design. The App is designed specifically for children. We do not knowingly collect, store, or process any personal data from children under the age of 13 (or any other relevant age under local law).

✅ No personal identifiers are ever transmitted

✅ No behavioral tracking or profiling

✅ Completely offline-first architecture

3. Data Collection and Usage

  • No Server Transfers: The App does not collect any personal information, and it does not transmit any data to external servers or third parties.
  • Local Storage Only: To provide a personalized game experience, the App allows the user to enter a player name and select a preferred language. This information is stored locally on your device only (using device preferences) and is used solely to display the player's name within the game and set the UI language. It never leaves your device.

📱 Everything stays on your device — no cloud, no servers, no surprise data sharing.

4. Third-Party Services & Advertising

The App does not include any third-party advertising, analytics tools, or internet-tracking software that could collect data about your usage. No SDKs, no trackers, no hidden telemetry.

🚫 Zero third-party libraries with network permission

🚫 No ads, no analytics, no push notification services

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect improvements or clarify our practices. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last updated" date at the top will be revised. We encourage parents to review this document periodically.
